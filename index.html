About Jewish Moments Project

It begins with you. For thousands of years the glue that binds Jewish tradition, value and life are the stories we tell. And now, in an era of all things digital, it's time we eternalize the Moments that comprise our interconnected Jewish narratives in what we're calling the Jewish Moments Project.

 

Throughout my life my Bubbe would share the Moments that make for the best stories. Like in the 1940s when my Papa ditched his prom date to ask my Bubbe to the dance. Or the Moment when my Bubbe asked G-d for a baby boy, and received twin girls instead. We all have these stories, and through the sharing of such Moments, we carry a responsibility to these stories that are deeply rooted and engrained in who we are as Jews and human beings. 

You might be asking: Well, wait - what makes a Moment "Jewish"? And to that, we respond with a smile and a nod. Every week we'll post a new prompt, and through the sharing of video anecdotes we aim to demonstrate the variety of different ways we can define, interpret and understand the Moments that are core to Judaism.

It's up to us to preserve these very stories that bind. And let’s start with you...
